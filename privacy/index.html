Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31st July 2025

1. Introduction

SwasthaIndia ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. By accessing or using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, age, gender, and address when you book a test.
  • Health-related Information: Information related to your health profile, selected tests, and medical reports.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device information.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on the site, and interaction with features.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage health tests and services
  • To confirm test bookings and appointments
  • To deliver reports and results to you securely
  • To respond to your inquiries and feedback
  • To send health-related reminders and promotional offers (with your consent)
  • To analyze site usage for improvement and better user experience

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Our Lab Partner: Atulaya Healthcare (NABL certified) to conduct the diagnostic tests and generate reports.
  • Service Providers: For IT support, cloud storage, and secure communication (e.g., WhatsApp API).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights or the safety of users.

5. Data Security

We take strong precautions to protect your data, including:

  • Secure HTTPS communication
  • Access control and encryption of sensitive information
  • Limited access to only authorized personnel

6. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather website analytics. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though it may limit some functionalities.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., WhatsApp, partner labs). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected inadvertently, we will promptly delete it.

9. Your Rights

  • Right to access, update or delete your information
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a regulatory authority

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users by posting an updated date on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
