About SwasthaIndia

Your trusted partner in preventive healthcare. We make diagnostic testing simple, affordable, and accessible to all.

Our Mission

At SwasthaIndia, our mission is to transform the way healthcare is delivered in India—starting with diagnostics. We aim to eliminate the barriers of time, distance, and affordability by bringing high-quality blood tests and full-body health checkups right to people’s doorsteps. Our focus is on building a system that is reliable, transparent, and patient-first, ensuring every Indian, regardless of location, can access timely diagnostic care without compromise.

By leveraging trusted lab partners using smart digital tools, we are creating a seamless experience—from booking to sample collection to getting reports—so that healthcare becomes more proactive, not reactive.

Who We Are
