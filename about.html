About Us

SwasthaIndia is your trusted health partner, bringing diagnostic care to your doorstep. Our mission is to simplify medical testing for every family across India by making it fast, affordable, and accessible from home.

We specialize in:

  • Blood test sample collection at home
  • Affordable health packages with reliable lab reports
  • CT scans, MRI, and other imaging test bookings
  • Doctor consultation and personalized health advice

Founded by Jai Yadav & Anoop Singh, SwasthaIndia is driven by the values of care, trust, and service. We believe everyone deserves access to quality diagnostics without the stress of traveling to a lab or hospital.
