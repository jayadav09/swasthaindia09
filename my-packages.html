Frequently Asked Questions

What are full body health packages?
Full body health packages are a comprehensive set of diagnostic tests designed to assess the overall health of your body. They help in the early detection of diseases and health conditions, allowing for timely medical intervention.
Is home sample collection free?
Yes, all our health packages include free home sample collection. Our certified phlebotomist will visit you at your preferred time and location to collect the sample.
How do I receive my test reports?
You will receive your reports digitally via email within the specified time. Hard copies can also be provided upon request for an additional charge.
Are the labs you partner with NABL accredited?
Yes, we only partner with top-tier, NABL-accredited diagnostic labs to ensure the highest standards of quality, accuracy, and reliability for all your test results.
